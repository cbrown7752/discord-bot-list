Make a discord bot course 1


Creating your Bot

1. Go to discord.com/developers

2. In the top right click new application

3. Create a new application

4. On the left hand side click bot

5. On the right hand side click add bot

6. Click copy token to copy your token

(Note your token should never be shared with anyone ever as this lets them gain access to your Discord bot)


Great now you have created your Discord bot profile on the page that you're currently on you can edit and customize your Discord bot profile


Inviting your Bot

1. On the right hand side go to OAurh2

2. Inside the scoops click the bot check mark

3.Scroll down and give your Bot all the permissions it will need

4. Scroll back up to the scoops area and copy the link that it gives you

(This is your invite link)
